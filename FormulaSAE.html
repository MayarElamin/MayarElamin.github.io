Formula SAE

I joined Formula SAE not because I was a lifelong motorsport enthusiast, but because I wanted to deepen my understanding of the design process behind high-performance vehicles. What began as curiosity quickly grew into admiration as I worked alongside incredibly knowledgeable teammates who pour their passion into every detail of the car. Being part of this environment has been both inspiring and motivating. It’s shown me how engineering, teamwork, and passion come together to create something extraordinary!

My Role

  • Performed carbon fiber layups using both dry and pre-preg methods, prepared epoxy resin, and performed vacuum bagging to manufacture aerodynamic components for Cyclone Racing’s CR-28 and CR-30.
  • Collaborated in fabrication and finishing processes by sanding molds and composite structures to achieve high surface quality and precise fit.
  • Managed material preparation by measuring and cutting carbon fiber, peel ply, breather cloth, and vacuum bags.
  • Developed and iterated side wing designs for the EV CR-30 and conducted CFD simulations to achieve 15 lbs of downforce and < 5 lbs of added drag.
