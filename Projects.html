Astrodynamics Space Mission

This project focused on planning an interplanetary mission from Earth to Venus using basic orbital mechanics. Our team designed and analyzed a Hohmann transfer, calculated required velocity changes (Δv), and analyzed the spacecraft’s orbits before departure and after arrival. Using MATLAB, we evaluated the required burns and time of flight to ensure the mission could reach Venus efficiently and within realistic fuel limits.
